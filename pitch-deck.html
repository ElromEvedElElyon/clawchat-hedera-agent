The Problem

AI agents cannot autonomously transact on decentralized infrastructure

No Agent-Scale Chain

Ethereum: $2-50 per tx. Bitcoin: 10-60 min. Neither was built for millions of micro-transactions between autonomous agents.

No Agent Discovery

No standard protocol for agents to find each other, negotiate services, or establish trust on-chain.

No Multi-LLM Routing

Agents are locked to single AI providers. When one fails, the entire workflow stops.

No Human Interface

Agent-to-agent systems lack transparency. Humans cannot observe or audit autonomous behavior.
